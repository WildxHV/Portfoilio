This page contains the case study of AI Arsenal: An AI Toolkit Website, which includes the Project Overview, Tools Used and Live Links to the official product.

Project Overview

I am thrilled to present the culmination of my efforts - AI Arsenal. This project was born out of the need to simplify the overwhelming landscape of AI tools.

With the AI Arsenal, users can bid farewell to the complexity of hunting for appropriate AI tools. This platform facilitates a streamlined journey, from exploration to selection, simplifying the decision-making process and fostering productivity.

Experience & Learning

During the development of this website, I gained valuable experience in web development, honing my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Django to create a captivating and secure AI Tools platform. As I focused on delivering a user-friendly interface, I delved into UX design principles, understanding user behavior to optimize navigation and enhance the overall experience.
